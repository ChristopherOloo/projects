ABOUT

The Dream Cars organisation is a company that deals with advertisement and supply of different brands of cars throughout the world.Our interest and target is that each individual in the world will be able access and have his/her own dream car.Our company deals mainly the third world countries and also nations which cars making companies like Toyotta have not reached.We supply cars from all the brands: from Toyotta, Mercedes, Ford,BMW and all others.We reduce the expences involved in importing new cars in the market and also their spearparts.We also supply those cars at reduced prices.

One thing to note is that for now we are mainly suppliers only and don't compete with the companies which have builded the cars present in the market today.But we have teams of engineers who are currently involved in creating new models of cars including sport cars for comfort and leisure to all our clients.You can also send to us the type car that you dream of riding and is not in the market together with all the specifications that you would like it to have though our contacts address.

Since most countries lack the supply of car spearparts especially those trending in the market, we took the initiative of supplying the spearparts to help them reduce the cost of importing the speearparts.This is a program which we believe that would help to reduce stress arising from inavailability of spearparts.

Apart from the cars and spearparts which we distribute we also have experts and engineers for repairing and maintenance of the cars and also advice on how the cars can be maximised on usage and also maintained.We have the plans for car racing competitions which will be held worldwide.Winners will get great recognition and also wonderful awards.
